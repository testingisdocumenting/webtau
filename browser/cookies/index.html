Add Cookies

browser.cookies.add("cookie-a", "hello") browser.cookies.add("cookie-b", "world")

Get Cookies

browser.cookies.get("cookie-a").should == "hello" browser.cookies.get("cookie-b").should == "world" browser.cookies.getAll().should == ["cookie-a": "hello", "cookie-b": "world"]

Delete Cookies

browser.cookies.delete("cookie-b") browser.cookies.deleteAll()
